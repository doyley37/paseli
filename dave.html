Who is Dave?

The brother that never was

During our trip to the Ape Caves our three daughters mentioned that they wish they had a brother. Finja joked that they did, but we lost him in the caves. "What? Who was that?" Avery asked. Finja explained that his name was "Dave", and while losing him was sad, we at least managed to bring 75% of our children home safely.

Now whenever we go anywhere, it's a running joke that we left Dave behind.

Dave is work a fiction. He's a product of our family's imagination. No Daves were harmed or lost in the production of this story.
